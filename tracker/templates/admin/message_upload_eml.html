This tool allows you to upload email messages in .eml format directly into the job tracker database.

  • Select an .eml file from your computer
  • The system will automatically extract the subject, sender, date, and body
  • Company identification and ML classification will be applied

Note: .eml files are raw email files that can be saved from most email clients (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
